My personal interests

My favorite hobby is playing video games... Yes I know that is very boring and typical of someone that is in the CS field
but hey something has to get us interested in tech. Video games for me is somehting that I can just get lost in and allow me
to capture other worlds and forget about daily stressors. Something interesting is that I do not play any multiplayer games,
I strictly like to find single player games that I can just take over as the character and make them me and enjoy the fun gameplay
or story. I have found it vary hard to find other people that share this similar interst in games. I swear everyone just only plays
the popular shooter or sports games, and I just don't get it.

My second favorite hobby is mountain biking. I actually didn't start biking until after I graduated high school and man was
I sad on what I had been missing out on. I find it really exciting to go out into the mountains and be away from most people and
technology. I find it really rewarding and calming to climb the mountain just deep in my own thought while getting a good workout.
Than the fun part is descending the mountain as fast as I can to test my skills. Overall it is by far my favorite hobby that makes
me feel good about myself instead of sitting inside on my computer at all times...

The last hobbie of mine that I will place in being one of my favorites is skiing. This is one that I grew up doing because it was one
of the main reasons my parents decided to live in Utah. I have been skiing since I was three years old and have been doing it as much as I
can every winter since. This becomes my get off the computer hobbie during the winter when I am not able to mountain bike. Skiing has become
more of my social hobbie that all of my friends also do, so most days it is just up in the mountains enjoying our time out there.

About Bryce

Hi my name is Bryce but my screen name for pretty much everything is Shashwamie. I couldn't tell you what that is or what it means, I came up with the word and has now just been me for over a decade. This site however I want to start building even futhur upon even after the homework assignment. This was a good start for me to be able to get my ideas visualized. I want to create a site that has all of Shashwamie's played games and games he plans to play. There is a couple of reasons that I wanted to start to build this list idea. First of all yes I am absolulty addicted to video games, but I do think that I manage it alright. Secondly I love to play such a wide variety of games that I can not keep track anymore of what games I played, beat, hated, or adored. So my main goal is to start to build this page to keep track of the games I play and my opinion about them. Lastly I want to have an upcoming page one day that will have a schedule of what I am playing in the future or tend to anyways. This list is already huge and I expect it to never stop growing.
